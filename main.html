Plot Summary

Inception is a mind-bending science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a professional thief who specializes in stealing secrets by infiltrating the subconscious during dreams. He's offered a chance to have his criminal record erased in exchange for performing an impossible task: inception — planting an idea into someone's mind.
